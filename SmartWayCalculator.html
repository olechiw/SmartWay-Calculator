Instructions

Ever wondered why so many companies have joined SmartWay? SmartWay carrier fleets measure and benchmark their performance and are placed into one of five performance ranks (Rank 1 is best.) By simply using SmartWay carriers, and especially better ranked SmartWay carriers, you can reduce pollution.

You can estimate the emissions savings you will achieve by using SmartWay carrier fleets with this calculator. Choose either the Basic version: you will choose a single SmartWay ranking for analysis, or Detailed: you will indicate the % of activity in each SmartWay rank.

​

This calculator will provide you with output estimates for Current and Goal emissions, either in grams or pounds which you can configure here.

​

This page requires javascript to function, without it enabled you will not be able to input emissions or obtain calculations.

Basic

To see your current emissions, input your current freight activity by choosing a carrier type(s) (18 choices), measurement units (miles or ton-miles) and input your activity. Then choose your current SmartWay performance level (1 through 5) or choose NON-SmartWay if you don’t use any SmartWay carriers. You’ll see your current emissions. Then, determine your goals (what-if scenario) by clicking on the Goal Tab.

Detailed

To see your current emissions, input your current freight activity by choosing a carrier type(s) (18 choices), measurement units (miles or ton-miles) and input your activity. Then input the percentage of carriers you use for each SmartWay Bin (1-5). If the carrier is not a SmartWay carrier count its percentage of activity as NON. You’ll see your current emissions. Then, determine your goals (what-if scenario) by clicking on the Goal Tab. This may include changing carrier types/quantities or improving your rankings.
